A Poem for You ❤️

Roses are red, violets are blue, ✿ My world seems brighter, all thanks to of you ✧ Your smile, your laughter, your beautiful heart, ❀ I cherish you deeply, never to part ✿
Just three months ago, our paths aligned ✧ A spark was lit, so warm, so kind ❀ Though miles apart on your special day, ✿ I wish I'd be with you — never away ✧
Tomorrow I'll see you, hold you near, ❀ Whisper sweet words, just in you to hear ✿ On your birthday, I send my love your way, ✧ Wishing you happiness, today and always! ❀
